Chocolate Chip Pancakes

Ingredients

  • ¾ cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons white vinegar
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ cup miniature chocolate chips
  • cooking spray

Steps

  • Step 1

    • Combine milk and vinegar, set aside for 5 mins.

  • Step 2

    • Combine flour, baking power, baking soda, and salt in a large bowl

  • Step 3

    • Add egg, butter, and vanilla extract to milk and vinegar mixture. Mix well. Then combine the dry flour mixture to the wet ingredients. Mix until combined. Fold in chocolate chips and allow batter to rest for 5 min.

  • Step 4

    • Heat large skilled over medium-low heat and coat with cooking spray. Pour ¼ cup batter into skillet. Cook until bubbles appear on surface, then flip. Cook the other side until brown. Repeat with remaining batter.
