Taco Meat

Ingredients

  • 1 lb lean ground beef
  • ½ teaspoon onion powder
  • ½ teaspoon garlic salt
  • ½ teaspoon celery salt
  • ½ teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 eight ounce can tomato sauce

Steps

  • Step 1

    • Heat large skillet over medium heat, pour in beef and cook until brown. Stir regularly

  • Step 2

    • Drain beef. Season with onion power, garlic and celery salt, and ground cumin. Pour tomato sauce over beef and stir until well mixed.

  • Step 3

    • Simmer beef on low heat for 5 minutes.
