Spicy Deviled Eggs

Ingrients

  • 12 eggs
  • 1 ½ tablespoons dijon mustard
  • 1 ½ tablespoons mayonnaise
  • ½ teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • ½ teaspoon hot pepper sauce
  • 1 pinch salt and black pepper
  • ¼ teaspoon ground paprika
  • 1 6oz can black olives, cut in half horizonatally

Steps

  1. Step 1

    2. Places eggs in a saucepan, bring to a boil, remove from heat and cover for 12 mins. Chill the eggs in cold water, then peel them.

  2. Step 2

    3. Cut each egg in half lengthwise. Remove the yolks and place them in a bowl with the mustard, mayonnaise, Worcestershire sauce, hot pepper sauce, salt, and pepper. Crush the yolkds and mix the ingredients thoroughly.

  3. Step 3

    4. Fill each egg half from step 2 with the yolk mixture, using a spoon or piping bag. Dust each egg with paprika and place an olive half in the center. Refrigerate until chilled, and serve.
